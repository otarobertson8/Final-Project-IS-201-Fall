PICKLEBALL PROS


Anna Leigh Waters

Anna Leigh Waters is an American professional pickleball player widely recognized as one of the top athletes in the sport. She turned professional at just 12 years old, becoming the youngest pro in pickleball history. Known for her fast-paced, powerful, and strategic playing style, Waters excels in singles, women’s doubles, and mixed doubles, often sweeping tournaments with “triple crown” victories. She frequently competes alongside her mother, Leigh Waters, forming a unique mother-daughter team on the professional circuit. Beyond her on-court success, Anna Leigh has become an ambassador for the sport, helping raise its profile and inspire young athletes worldwide. Her achievements and dedication continue to redefine competitive pickleball and influence the next generation of players.



Federico Staksrud

Federico Staksrud is a professional pickleball player from Buenos Aires, Argentina, who has rapidly risen to become one of the sport’s top competitors. As of 2025, he holds the #1 ranking on the PPA Tour in both men’s singles and men’s doubles — a rare feat demonstrating his versatility and dominance. Known for a powerful baseline‑driven playing style, Staksrud blends aggressive g roundstrokes with smart positioning and strong net play. He began his sporting career in tennis, then transitioned to pickleball in 2021, leveraging his athletic background and competitive mindset to quickly climb the pro ranks. Off the court, he’s respected for his sportsmanship and strategic approach to the game, and his rapid success has helped raise the profile of international players in professional pickleball.
